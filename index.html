Introduction

I have taken images from Google's street view car mounted 360 camera and tried to present them as works of art in their own right. In this way I am asking questions about authorship, editing, and technology.

There are technical considerations when selecting and editing images, such as:

  1. blurring of human figures and certain areas in the images
  2. 'fish eye' distortion
  3. the Google watermark peppered through the images
  4. the relatively lo-res qulait of the captured images

These considerations have given interesting limitations and restrictions on which images are selected for inclusion in this project
